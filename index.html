Our Preschool Programs (18 Months – 6.5 Years)

I

Pre-KG

18 Months – 2.5 Years

Focus on social skills, basic language sounds, rhymes and play learning. Children develop comfort and confidence in their first learning environment.

II

LKG

2.5 – 4 Years

ABCD learning, phonics, numbers, rhymes, storytelling and activity-based learning that develops creativity, curiosity and communication skills.

III

UKG

4 – 6.5 Years

Reading, writing, math basics, spoken language skills and school readiness to prepare children confidently for primary school education.
