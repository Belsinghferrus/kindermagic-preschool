Admission Steps

Simple Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to be simple and parent-friendly. Follow the steps below to enroll your child.

Step 1 – Visit School

Parents are welcome to visit our campus, explore the classrooms and meet our teachers to understand the learning environment.

🏫
📝

Step 2 – Fill Enquiry Form

Fill the enquiry form with basic details so our team can guide you through the admission process.

Step 3 – Submit Documents

Submit the required documents including the child’s birth certificate and identification details.

📄
✅

Step 4 – Confirm Admission

Once documents are verified, complete the admission confirmation and welcome your child to our preschool.
