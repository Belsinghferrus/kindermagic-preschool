🎓 Preschool Academic Programs

Our preschool curriculum focuses on structured learning blended with play-based exploration. Each level gradually prepares children with academic readiness, social interaction and emotional development.

Playgroup

Children are introduced to a joyful learning environment where they explore colors, shapes, music, movement and group interaction. Activities focus on sensory learning and emotional comfort.

Nursery

Nursery builds the foundation for language and number recognition. Children learn alphabets, rhymes, storytelling, early writing strokes and structured play activities.

Junior KG

At this stage children develop reading readiness, basic math concepts, creative expression, memory skills and communication abilities through interactive activities.

Senior KG

Senior KG prepares children for primary school with structured learning in reading, writing, phonics, numbers, problem solving and group projects.

📚 Free Take-Home Library

Our weekly library program encourages children to explore books beyond the classroom and develop strong reading habits early in life.

Improve Reading Habit

Children take home storybooks every week which helps them build regular reading practice with their parents.

Build Vocabulary

Exposure to different stories introduces new words and improves language development naturally.

Develop Imagination

Storybooks stimulate creativity and help children imagine different characters, places and ideas.
